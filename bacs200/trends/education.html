Education Web Trends

By Conner Hubbell on October 15, 2021

There have been some major changes in web trends having to do with education in these past few years for many reasons. Online education was already a pretty sizeable industry but with the quarantines and lockdowns that the COVID-19 pandemic caused, with schools being shut down and forced quickly into this online format of education. Web trends had to adapt quickly and some services like Zoom and Microsoft Teams exploded in popularity and many other online education resources have been created in their wake. Online education went from sort of a niche area to becoming something millions of students relied on daily to get their education since in person learning was not possible with the pandemic. Teachers also had to adapt to this new format of teaching and it shook up the whole landscape of education. Web development had to keep up with this new influx of demand for online education platforms and develop new tools that allowed teachers to convey information to their students in the best way possible.
